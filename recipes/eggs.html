Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Butter or Olive oil

Cooking instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a mixing bowl
  2. Stir eggs with a fork until consistent
  3. Add black pepper to eggs and stir
  4. Put frying pan on stove and turn heat to high
  5. Add butter or oil to the pan and wait for it to sizzle
  6. Pour eggs into pan and move them around with a spatula to cook evenly
