Ramen

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Flavor packet
  • Any aditional items you want (Shrimp, veggies, so on)

Cooking instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil
  2. Put the noodles in the pot along with any other items
  3. Let it boil until noodles are soft and any additonal items are cooked
  4. Drain out excesse water and turn off heat
  5. Add flavor packet and stir
